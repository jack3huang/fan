About Mockingjay Tech

Smart Factory Automation / QC / Scale

10+ years manufacturing excellence from Shenzhen's heart — building the world's most innovative portable fans.

A Factory Built on Innovation

Founded in 2015 in Longhua, Shenzhen, Mockingjay Tech set out with a single ambition: to build the most advanced portable fan that buyers around the world could trust. Today, we're a team of 300+ engineers, designers, and manufacturing specialists operating from a 4,000㎡ state-of-the-art facility.

What started as a small R&D workshop has grown into a full-stack manufacturing operation — from our in-house mold studio and precision motor assembly line to our 6-layer quality control process and global logistics team.

Our flagship product, the JF168 with 199 adjustable speed levels and a 9000mAh battery, represents everything we believe about portable cooling: intelligent control, long-lasting power, and beautiful design in your hand.

Our Journey

2015

Company Founded

Mockingjay Tech established in Longhua, Shenzhen. Initial team of 30 engineers focused on portable fan R&D and mold development.

2017

CE Certification Achieved

Obtained CE certification for the European market, opening doors to wholesale distributors across the EU and UK.

2019

Factory Expansion

Moved to our current 4,000㎡ facility. Added in-house mold workshop and expanded production capacity to 10M units/year.

2021

OEM/ODM Division Launch

Launched a dedicated OEM/ODM division to serve brand owners and private label importers. Minimum order 500 pcs with full customization.

2023

JF168 — 199-Speed Innovation

Released the JF168 with breakthrough 199 adjustable speed levels and 9000mAh battery — instantly becoming our bestselling export product.

2025

30M Annual Capacity

Reached 30 million units/year production capacity. Serving 50+ countries across e-commerce, retail, and private label markets.
