Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Standard MOQ is 500 units per model/color. For Amazon or small business buyers, we can sometimes accommodate 200–300 pcs. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
How long does production take?
Standard models: 15–20 business days after deposit confirmed. Custom OEM orders with mold or packaging changes: 25–35 days. Sample orders: 3–5 days.
Can I get a sample before placing an order?
Yes. Standard samples are available at sample cost + shipping. Sample fee is refundable when you place a bulk order of 500+ pcs. Contact Sam for current sample pricing.
Do your products have CE certification?
Yes — all portable fans are CE certified for European Union market entry. We can provide certification documentation with your order. Other certifications available on request.
What customization options are available?
We offer: custom colors (Pantone matching), private label packaging, logo printing on product, custom motor speed/firmware, and full mold redesign for ODM orders. See our OEM/ODM page for details.
What payment terms do you accept?
Standard terms: 30% T/T deposit, 70% before shipment. For established partners: 30% deposit, 70% against B/L copy. L/C accepted for orders above $50,000 USD.
How do you ship orders?
FOB Shenzhen is standard. We can arrange DDP door-to-door delivery for most countries. We work with DHL, FedEx, UPS (small orders), and major freight forwarders for sea freight.
What warranty do products come with?
12 months warranty from ship date. Defective units below 2% are replaced at cost in the next order. We maintain QC records for each batch for 3 years.
