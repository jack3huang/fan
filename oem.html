Our OEM/ODM Process

01
Consultation
Share your requirements — quantity, features, target market, budget. We advise the best approach.
02
Quotation
Detailed quote within 24h — unit price, tooling cost (if any), sample cost, and lead time.
03
Sample
Physical sample produced (3–15 days depending on complexity) for your approval before mass production.
04
Production
Mass production begins after deposit confirmed. QC reports and production photos sent at each milestone.
05
Delivery
Pre-shipment inspection, packing, and logistics. FOB Shenzhen or door-to-door DDP available.
